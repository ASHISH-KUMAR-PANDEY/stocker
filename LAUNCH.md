## Q&A to have ready (they will come up)
- **Gamifying investing?** → The swipe is *discovery* — it never places a trade. Investing is a separate, deliberate step; it rewards learning, not trading frequency.
- **Is it advice / SEBI-compliant?** → Discovery & education, not advice. The score is a *similarity* signal, never "buy this," no return projections. You trade through your own broker; we never hold money.
- **Can I invest real money?** → Yes — self-host and connect your own Zerodha (single-account today). The hosted demo is fully simulated.
