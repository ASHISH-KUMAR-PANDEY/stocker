How it works

Research turned into a habit — the fun part of investing, before any money is on the line.

1

Tell it your type

A 30-second quiz — your risk, style, sectors, values. No jargon.

2

Swipe to discover

A personalized deck with a transparent "your-type score," a real 90-day chart, and plain-English green/red flags.

3

Build a portfolio

Matches become a paper portfolio. When you're ready, invest for real through your own broker.

What's inside

Real data, an honest score, and a clean line between discovery and your money.

🔥

Swipe-first discovery

Right = like, left = pass, ★ = watch. Discovery that feels like a feed, not a screener.

✦

Your-type score

A transparent similarity score — not a buy rating — with a one-line "why."

📈

Real NSE data

Live end-of-day prices, 90-day charts, computed volatility/momentum, real news headlines — free.

📊

Paper portfolio

Track matches with P&L, allocation, cash and buy/sell — play money by default.

🔗

Bring your own broker

Connect your own Zerodha and place real basket orders. Stocker never holds your money.

🛡️

Safe by design

The swipe never buys. A cost/funds guard and a spend cap stand between you and a mistap.

Run your own in a minute

It's one Python app. Demo mode needs no keys and no money.

# clone & run the safe demo
git clone https://github.com/ASHISH-KUMAR-PANDEY/stocker.git
cd stocker
python3 -m pip install -r requirements.txt
python3 server.py   # → http://127.0.0.1:4173

⚠️ Educational project — not investment advice

Stocker is an open, self-hosted, educational tool. It is not a brokerage and does not hold your money or securities. You run your own copy and are solely responsible for your own trades and any losses. No fractional shares in India — the smallest real buy is one whole share. Company info is illustrative; prices, charts and news are real. Comply with your broker's terms and local regulations.
